Our Work

Spay and Neuter

The primary goal of SPCA is animal birth control. We have been conducting street animal birth control campaing on monthly or weekly basis.

Feeding Campaign

During the global Pandemic, our team members and volunteers used to feed the street animals on weekly basis.

Rescue

We have been tirelessly rescuing street animals which suffer from mange, cancer, hit and run case or heinous cases of human abuse.

Advocacy

Animals are sentient beings. This means that they are capable of feeling. We provide animal advocay whenever it's needed.

Adoption

We urge pet lovers to take an action to save lives and bring hope for our furry babies in need

Treatment

We provide treatment and follow up to our rescued animals or as per request.
