  • Donate

    Via Paypal or eSewa or donate food and necessary items to our dogs in the shelter

  • Spread the Word

    Share about us in the social media

  • Adopt

    Adopt Don't Shop; Just like us they want to know love before they die

Why Help Us?

The condition of stray animals in the street of Kathmandu and other places is horrible. These hungry animals can't speak for themselves thus we need to safeguard them. Protecting them is something we humans should take pride in wholeheartedly, it is our responsibility. They surely deserve to live their lives free from suffering and exploitation. We are passionate animals lovers and have been giving services to our best ability.
